📋 Rules & Notes

  • Hard return: Start heading back at 3:15 PM. Ship deadline 4:00 PM. No debate.
  • Public Gardens: No bikes inside. Lock up outside.
  • Point Pleasant Park: Bikes OK on main roads/pathways (weekday). Be respectful around walkers.
  • Weather: Warm, mostly dry. Bring water. Halifax fog can roll in fast in the afternoon.
  • Citadel hill: Real climb on a bike. Skip if energy is low.
  • Cell service: May be spotty in Point Pleasant Park.
